Welcome

Manchester United Football Club was founded in 1878 and is one of the most successful football clubs in the world. The club plays its home games at Old Trafford in Manchester, England.

Fans across the globe support the Red Devils for their history, spirit, and unforgettable moments in world football.

Use the navigation above to explore the team roster, fixtures, and ways to contact us.

Why Are They Called “The Red Devils”?

The nickname “The Red Devils” came from the club’s bold red uniforms and fierce reputation in the 1950s and 60s. The name was popularized by then-manager Sir Matt Busby, who wanted a powerful image for the team. It has since become one of the most recognizable nicknames in sports.

Trophies and Achievements

Premier League Titles

Manchester United have won the English top-flight league title a record 20 times, including 13 Premier League titles since 1992.

Champions League Wins

The club has lifted the UEFA Champions League trophy 3 times — in 1968, 1999, and 2008 — representing the very best in European football.

Visit Old Trafford

The “Theatre of Dreams,” as it’s known, is located in Greater Manchester. You can visit the stadium, museum, and club megastore year-round.
