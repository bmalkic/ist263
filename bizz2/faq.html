Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for business owners thinking about ads.

Common Questions

What platforms do you work with?

We mainly work with Meta (Facebook and Instagram), Google Ads, and TikTok. If you use something else, we can talk about it on the strategy call.

How much ad budget do I need?

It depends on your industry, but many small businesses start with a test budget and then scale up once we see what works.

Do you lock me into a long contract?

No long-term lock-ins. We believe people stay because the work helps, not because they are stuck in a contract.

Can you fix my existing ad account?

Yes. We can review your current campaigns, find clear problems, and either fix them or give you a list of changes to make.

Do you also redesign websites?

We focus on ads first, but we can give simple feedback on landing pages and basic changes that may help conversion.

How do we get started?

Go to the Home page and fill out the short form. We will follow up with a free ad strategy call.
