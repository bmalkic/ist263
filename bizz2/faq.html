Freelancers like designers, developers, writers, and marketers who want a clearer brand and better clients.

Do I need to be full–time?

No. You can be part–time or full–time. The key is that you are serious about improving your freelance business.

It depends on your starting point, but many freelancers notice clearer branding and better client conversations within 4–8 weeks.

That is normal. Part of this work is testing and choosing a direction based on your skills, interests, and the market.
