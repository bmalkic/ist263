Recommended Tools

These are common tools many freelancers use to stay organized:

  • Notion or Google Docs – for notes, systems, and client info.
  • Canva – for creating simple graphics and visuals.
  • Calendly – for booking calls without back–and–forth emails.
  • Google Calendar – for planning deep work and client time.
